Welcome to the Anonymous Credentials Meeting!

Find me on github!

This is a workshop to be held at PETS2021 on July.

What is the topic? The topic is Anonymous Credentials, a construction that tries to solve the problem of needing to propagate trust signals while protecting anonymity.

This form takes inspiration from the pl-hci-school-2020 organized by Sarah Chasins, Elena Glassman, Shriram Krishnamurthi and Hila Peleg, and by the Fourteenth Algorithmic Number Theory Symposium, ANTS-XIV organized by Steven Galbraith.
