Welcome to the Anonymous Credentials Meeting!

Find me on github!

This is an informal meeting to be held at Real World Crypto 2021.

What is an informal meeting? It is a far-less planned meeting where people interested in the topic come together and discuss ideas!

What is the topic? The topic is Anonymous Credentials, a construction that tries to solve the problem of needing to propagate trust signals while protecting anonymity.

This form takes inspiration from the pl-hci-school-2020 organized by Sarah Chasins, Elena Glassman, Shriram Krishnamurthi and Hila Peleg, and by the Fourteenth Algorithmic Number Theory Symposium, ANTS-XIV organized by Steven Galbraith.

Information

The event will be entirely virtual.

The meeting will happen for at least 30 min but can go for as long as wanted.

Schedule

There is no schedule set up in stone, but the main ideas to discuss are:

  • Privacy Pass: its status and related ideas
  • Other use cases for anonymous credentials
  • Adding metadata to anonymous credentials
  • External verifiability of anonymous credentials.
